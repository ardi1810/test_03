Best Places To Live In Virginia Beach In 2023

Introduction

Virginia Beach is a popular tourist destination known for its beautiful beaches, boardwalk, and lively nightlife. But it's not just a great place to visit, it's also a great place to live. With its mild climate, excellent schools, and diverse neighborhoods, Virginia Beach offers something for everyone. In this article, we'll explore the best places to live in Virginia Beach in 2023.

1. Sandbridge

Located on the southern end of Virginia Beach, Sandbridge is a quiet, secluded community known for its pristine beaches and wildlife refuge. It's the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy a more relaxed way of life. With its large beachfront homes and spacious lots, Sandbridge is ideal for families looking for a peaceful retreat.

2. Great Neck

Great Neck is a popular neighborhood located in the heart of Virginia Beach. It's known for its excellent schools, tree-lined streets, and upscale shopping and dining options. With its close proximity to the beach and easy access to major highways, Great Neck offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and beachside living.

3. Oceanfront

If you're looking for a lively, bustling community with plenty of entertainment options, Oceanfront is the place for you. Located along the boardwalk, Oceanfront is home to a variety of restaurants, shops, and nightlife venues. It's also the perfect place to enjoy beach activities like surfing, swimming, and volleyball.

4. Hilltop

Hilltop is a diverse neighborhood located in the center of Virginia Beach. It's known for its excellent schools, affordable housing, and wide range of shopping and dining options. With its proximity to major highways and easy access to the beach, Hilltop is a great option for families and young professionals looking for a convenient, affordable place to live.

5. Red Mill Commons

Red Mill Commons is a newer development located in the southern end of Virginia Beach. It's known for its modern, upscale shopping and dining options and convenient location near major highways. With its mix of townhomes, condos, and single-family homes, Red Mill Commons offers a wide range of housing options for families and young professionals.

6. Pungo

Pungo is a rural community located in the southern end of Virginia Beach. It's known for its farms, orchards, and equestrian activities. With its quiet, peaceful way of life and wide-open spaces, Pungo is the perfect place to escape the city and enjoy a more rural way of life.

7. Kempsville

Kempsville is a historic neighborhood located in the center of Virginia Beach. It's known for its tree-lined streets, historic homes, and excellent schools. With its close proximity to major highways and easy access to the beach, Kempsville offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and suburban charm.

8. Princess Anne

Princess Anne is a newer development located in the southern end of Virginia Beach. It's known for its modern, upscale homes and convenient location near major highways. With its mix of townhomes, condos, and single-family homes, Princess Anne offers a wide range of housing options for families and young professionals.

9. North End

The North End is a quiet, exclusive community located on the northern end of Virginia Beach. It's known for its beautiful beaches, large beachfront homes, and secluded way of life. With its proximity to the beach and easy access to major highways, the North End offers the perfect balance of luxury living and beachside charm.

10. Chesapeake Bay

If you're looking for a waterfront community with plenty of outdoor activities, Chesapeake Bay is the place for you. Located on the eastern edge of Virginia Beach, Chesapeake Bay is known for its boating, fishing, and water sports. With its mix of waterfront homes and wooded lots, Chesapeake Bay offers a unique blend of outdoor living and modern convenience.

Conclusion

Whether you're looking for a quiet, secluded retreat or a bustling, urban neighborhood, Virginia Beach has something for everyone. With its mild climate, excellent schools, and wide range of housing options, Virginia Beach is the perfect place to call home. So why wait? Start exploring these top neighborhoods in Virginia Beach and find your perfect place to live in 2023.
