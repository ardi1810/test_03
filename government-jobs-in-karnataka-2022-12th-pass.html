Government Jobs In Karnataka 2022 For 12Th Pass

Introduction

Karnataka is a state in India that offers a wide range of government job opportunities for those who have passed their 12th standard. The state government releases notifications every year for various posts in different departments. In this article, we will discuss the different government job opportunities available for 12th pass students in Karnataka in 2022.

1. Karnataka State Police

Karnataka State Police is one of the largest police forces in India. They release notifications for the recruitment of Constables, Armed Police Constables, and Sub-Inspectors. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a physical standard test, physical endurance test, written examination, and an interview.

2. Karnataka Postal Circle

Karnataka Postal Circle releases notifications for the recruitment of Postal Assistants, Sorting Assistants, and Multi-Tasking Staff. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a written examination and an interview.

3. Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation

Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation releases notifications for the recruitment of Driver and Conductor posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a driving test, written examination, and an interview.

4. Karnataka Forest Department

Karnataka Forest Department releases notifications for the recruitment of Forest Guards and Forest Watchers. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a physical standard test, physical endurance test, written examination, and an interview.

5. Karnataka State Tourism Development Corporation

Karnataka State Tourism Development Corporation releases notifications for the recruitment of Assistant Cook and Room Boy posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a written examination and an interview.

6. Karnataka State Warehousing Corporation

Karnataka State Warehousing Corporation releases notifications for the recruitment of Assistant, Manager, and Technical Assistant posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a written examination and an interview.

7. Karnataka State Pollution Control Board

Karnataka State Pollution Control Board releases notifications for the recruitment of Assistant Environment Officer and Junior Scientific Officer posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a written examination and an interview.

8. Karnataka State Agricultural Produce Processing and Export Corporation Limited

Karnataka State Agricultural Produce Processing and Export Corporation Limited releases notifications for the recruitment of Junior Engineer and Assistant Manager posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a written examination and an interview.

9. Karnataka State Fire and Emergency Services

Karnataka State Fire and Emergency Services release notifications for the recruitment of Fireman and Fireman Driver posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a physical standard test, physical endurance test, written examination, and an interview.

10. Karnataka State Health and Family Welfare Society

Karnataka State Health and Family Welfare Society releases notifications for the recruitment of Junior Health Assistants and Junior Health Inspectors. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is 12th pass. The selection process includes a written examination and an interview.

Conclusion

There are various government job opportunities available for 12th pass students in Karnataka. The candidates need to keep a lookout for the notifications released by different departments and start preparing accordingly. With hard work and dedication, one can secure a good government job in the state of Karnataka.
