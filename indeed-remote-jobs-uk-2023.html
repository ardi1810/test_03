Introduction

The past year has completely changed the way we work. With the pandemic forcing companies to shift to remote work, the demand for remote jobs has skyrocketed. The good news is, there are plenty of remote jobs available in the UK, and with the help of Indeed, finding them has become easier than ever. In this article, we’ll take a look at how Indeed can help you find remote jobs in the UK in 2023, and some tips on how to make the most out of your job search.

What is Indeed?

Indeed is one of the largest job search engines in the world, with a presence in over 60 countries. The platform allows job seekers to search for jobs by location, industry, job title, and more. What’s great about Indeed is that it aggregates job postings from multiple sources, including company websites, job boards, and recruitment agencies, making it a one-stop-shop for job seekers.

Using Indeed to Find Remote Jobs in the UK

To find remote jobs on Indeed, simply type “remote” or “work from home” in the “what” search bar, and your preferred location in the “where” search bar. You can also filter your search by job type, industry, salary, and more. Once you find a job that interests you, click on the job title to read more about the job description and requirements.

Tips for Finding Remote Jobs on Indeed

1. Use specific keywords: Instead of using generic terms like “remote jobs”, use specific keywords like “remote customer service jobs” or “remote software developer jobs”. This will help you find more relevant job postings. 2. Set up job alerts: Indeed allows you to set up job alerts for specific keywords and locations. This means that you will receive an email notification every time a new job posting that matches your criteria is added. 3. Customise your resume: When applying for remote jobs, it’s important to customise your resume to highlight your remote work experience and skills. Use keywords from the job posting in your resume and cover letter to increase your chances of getting noticed. 4. Network: Just because you’re working remotely doesn’t mean you can’t network. Use LinkedIn and other social media platforms to connect with professionals in your industry and to find out about job opportunities.

Conclusion

Finding remote jobs in the UK has never been easier, thanks to Indeed. With a few simple tips and tricks, you can make the most out of your job search and increase your chances of finding the perfect remote job. So, what are you waiting for? Start your job search on Indeed today!
