Job Openings At Tulsa Public Schools

Introduction

Tulsa Public Schools is one of the largest school districts in Oklahoma, with over 41,000 students attending its 86 schools. The district is committed to hiring talented and passionate educators who can help shape the lives of Tulsa's youth. In this article, we will discuss the job openings at Tulsa Public Schools in 2023 and what you need to know to apply.

Teaching Positions

Tulsa Public Schools is currently hiring teachers for various subjects, including English, math, science, and social studies. Teachers are responsible for creating lesson plans, grading assignments, and providing feedback to students. Applicants must have a valid teaching certificate issued by the Oklahoma State Department of Education and a bachelor's degree in education or a related field.

Administrative Positions

The district is also hiring for administrative positions, such as principals, assistant principals, and district-level administrators. These positions require a master's degree in education, business administration, or a related field. The ideal candidate should have several years of experience in education and leadership roles.

Support Staff Positions

Tulsa Public Schools is also hiring for support staff positions, such as custodians, bus drivers, and cafeteria workers. These positions require a high school diploma or equivalent and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. Applicants must also be able to pass a background check and drug test.

How to Apply

To apply for a job at Tulsa Public Schools, visit the district's website and click on the "Careers" tab. You will be directed to a page where you can create an account and submit your application. Make sure to read the job description carefully and tailor your resume and cover letter to the position you are applying for.

Benefits

Tulsa Public Schools offers a comprehensive benefits package to its employees, including health insurance, retirement plans, and tuition reimbursement. The district also provides ongoing professional development opportunities to help teachers and staff grow in their careers.

Conclusion

Working at Tulsa Public Schools is a rewarding experience that allows you to make a positive impact on young people's lives. Whether you're a teacher, administrator, or support staff member, there are many job opportunities available. If you're interested in joining the Tulsa Public Schools team, visit the district's website and start your application today!
