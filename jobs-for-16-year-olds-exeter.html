Introduction

Are you a 16-year-old living in Exeter and looking for a job? Well, the good news is that there are plenty of opportunities available for you. Whether you are looking for a part-time job to earn some extra cash or want to gain some work experience, there are many options out there. In this article, we will explore some of the best jobs for 16 year olds in Exeter in 2023.

Retail Industry

The retail industry is a great place to start when it comes to finding a job as a 16 year old. Many stores in Exeter are looking for part-time staff to help with customer service, stocking shelves, and cash handling. Some of the popular stores that hire 16 year olds include Primark, H&M, and Debenhams.

Hospitality Industry

The hospitality industry is another popular option for 16 year olds in Exeter. Restaurants, cafes, and bars are always in need of part-time staff to help with serving customers, clearing tables, and washing dishes. Some of the popular places to work in the hospitality industry include Starbucks, Costa Coffee, and Pizza Hut.

Supermarkets and Grocery Stores

Another option for 16 year olds in Exeter is to work in a supermarket or grocery store. Many supermarkets such as Asda, Sainsbury's, and Tesco hire 16 year olds to work as part-time staff. Roles include assisting with stock replenishment, customer service, and cash handling.

Work Experience Opportunities

If you are looking to gain some work experience, there are plenty of opportunities available in Exeter. Many local businesses offer work experience placements for 16 year olds, which can be a great way to gain valuable skills and experience in your chosen field. Some of the popular industries for work experience include healthcare, law, and finance.

Online Jobs

In today's digital age, there are many online jobs available for 16 year olds in Exeter. These include roles such as online tutoring, freelance writing, and social media management. These jobs can be done from the comfort of your own home, making them a convenient option for those who need to balance work with school.

Volunteering

Volunteering is another great way to gain work experience and make a difference in your community. There are many charities in Exeter that are looking for volunteers to help with fundraising, event planning, and administration. Volunteering can also be a great way to meet new people and make new friends.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many job opportunities available for 16 year olds in Exeter in 2023. Whether you are looking for a part-time job to earn some extra cash, want to gain work experience, or simply want to make a difference in your community, there are plenty of options out there. So, why not start your job search today and take the first step towards building your career?
