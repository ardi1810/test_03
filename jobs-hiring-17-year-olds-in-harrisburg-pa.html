Jobs Hiring 17 Year Olds In Harrisburg Pa

Introduction

Are you a 17-year-old in Harrisburg, PA, looking for a job? Well, you're in luck! There are plenty of job opportunities available for you in the city. This article will explore some of the best jobs hiring 17-year-olds in Harrisburg, PA, and provide tips on how to land the perfect job.

Food Service Industry

One of the best industries to look for jobs in Harrisburg, PA, is the food service industry. Many restaurants and cafes are always in need of people to take orders, prepare food, and clean up. Some of the well-known food chains like McDonald's, Burger King, and Dunkin Donuts are always hiring 17-year-olds. You can also check out local cafes and restaurants in your area.

Retail Industry

Another industry where you can find jobs is the retail industry. Many stores and shopping malls in Harrisburg, PA, are looking for part-time employees. You can work as a sales associate, cashier, or stocker. Some of the stores that hire 17-year-olds include Target, Walmart, and Kohl's.

Summer Camps

If you enjoy working with kids, you can look for jobs at summer camps in Harrisburg, PA. Many summer camps hire 17-year-olds as counselors, lifeguards, or activity coordinators. Some of the popular summer camps in the area include Camp Curtin YMCA, Capital Area YMCA, and Camp Thompson.

Babysitting and Pet Sitting

Babysitting and pet sitting are also great job opportunities for 17-year-olds in Harrisburg, PA. Many families in the area are looking for someone to take care of their children or pets while they are away. You can advertise your services on social media platforms or register on websites like Care.com.

Delivery Services

If you have a reliable vehicle and a valid driver's license, you can work for delivery services like UberEats, DoorDash, or GrubHub. These services are always looking for part-time delivery drivers. You can earn money by delivering food orders and groceries to customers in your area.

Job Searching Tips

When looking for a job as a 17-year-old in Harrisburg, PA, here are some tips to keep in mind:
  • Check online job boards like Indeed, Monster, and Glassdoor for job postings.
  • Visit local businesses and ask if they have any open positions.
  • Prepare a resume and cover letter to showcase your skills and experience.
  • Dress appropriately for job interviews.
  • Be enthusiastic and show a willingness to learn.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are plenty of job opportunities available for 17-year-olds in Harrisburg, PA. You can work in the food service industry, retail industry, summer camps, babysitting, pet sitting, or delivery services. Keep in mind the job searching tips mentioned above to increase your chances of landing the perfect job. Good luck with your job search!
