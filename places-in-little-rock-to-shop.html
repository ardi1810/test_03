Shop till you drop in Little Rock!

If you're looking for a shopping experience that's second to none, Little Rock is the place to be. With a wide range of stores and boutiques, you'll find everything you need and more. From high-end fashion to unique vintage finds, Little Rock has it all. Let's take a look at the top places to shop in Little Rock.

The Promenade at Chenal

The Promenade at Chenal is a premier outdoor shopping destination located in West Little Rock. With over 50 stores and restaurants, you can spend the whole day browsing and dining. From popular retailers like Anthropologie and Lululemon to local boutiques, you'll find something for everyone.

The Heights

The Heights is a charming neighborhood in Little Rock that's known for its unique shops and boutiques. From vintage clothing to handmade crafts, you'll find a variety of treasures in this area. Be sure to check out Domestic Domestic for stylish home goods and clothing, and Box Turtle for one-of-a-kind gifts and accessories.

River Market District

The River Market District is a vibrant area in downtown Little Rock that's home to a variety of shops and restaurants. From the bustling Ottenheimer Market Hall to the eclectic Little Rock Farmer's Market, there's always something to see and do in this area. Be sure to stop by Bella Vita for stylish home decor and the Arkansas Arts Center Museum Shop for unique gifts.

Hillcrest

Hillcrest is a historic neighborhood in Little Rock that's known for its charming homes and eclectic shops. From vintage clothing to handmade jewelry, you'll find a variety of unique items in this area. Be sure to check out Haus Werk for stylish home decor and clothing, and Bang-Up Betty for fun and quirky jewelry.

Park Plaza Mall

Park Plaza Mall is a popular indoor shopping destination located in midtown Little Rock. With over 80 stores, you'll find a wide range of retailers, including Dillard's and J.C. Penney. Be sure to stop by Altar'd State for trendy clothing and accessories, and Sephora for all your beauty needs.

The Outlets of Little Rock

If you're looking for great deals on designer brands, the Outlets of Little Rock is the place to be. With over 70 stores, you'll find a variety of retailers, including Nike, Kate Spade, and Michael Kors. Be sure to stop by the Coach Outlet for stylish bags and accessories, and the Sunglass Hut for designer shades.

The Bernice Garden Farmers Market

The Bernice Garden Farmers Market is a popular outdoor market that's held on Sundays in downtown Little Rock. With a variety of vendors selling fresh produce, handmade crafts, and artisanal foods, it's a great place to shop and support local businesses.

The Green Corner Store

The Green Corner Store is a unique shop in downtown Little Rock that specializes in eco-friendly products and locally made goods. From organic skincare to handmade soaps, you'll find a variety of sustainable products in this store. Be sure to check out their selection of books and gifts as well.

The Bookstore at Library Square

If you're a book lover, the Bookstore at Library Square is a must-visit destination in Little Rock. Located in the Central Arkansas Library System's main library, this bookstore offers a wide range of books and gifts. Be sure to check out their selection of local authors and Arkansas-themed gifts.

The Painted Pig

The Painted Pig is a fun and creative pottery studio in Little Rock that's perfect for all ages. With a variety of pottery pieces to choose from, you can create your own unique masterpiece. Be sure to check out their selection of glazes and tools, and don't forget to take home your finished piece. In conclusion, Little Rock has a variety of shopping destinations that are perfect for every style and budget. Whether you're looking for high-end fashion or unique vintage finds, you'll find everything you need and more in Little Rock. So, what are you waiting for? Start shopping!
