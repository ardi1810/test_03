Services Offered by Security Companies

Security companies in Brooklyn Pretoria offer a range of services tailored to meet the specific needs of their clients. Some of the services offered include:

Armed Guards

Armed guards are trained professionals who are licensed to carry firearms. They provide physical security and deter criminal activities on your property. Their presence alone is often enough to deter intruders from attempting to break into your property.

CCTV Surveillance

CCTV surveillance involves the use of cameras placed strategically around your property to monitor and record any suspicious activities. This is an effective way of deterring criminal activities and providing evidence in the event of a break-in.

Alarm Systems

Alarm systems are designed to alert you and the security company in the event of an attempted break-in. They can be connected to a monitoring center that will dispatch armed guards to your property immediately.

24/7 Monitoring

Some security companies offer 24/7 monitoring services. This means that your property is being monitored around the clock, and any suspicious activities are reported to the security company immediately.
