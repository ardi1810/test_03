Introduction

Dubai is one of the fastest-growing cities in the world, and it is attracting a lot of people from different countries. Due to the increasing population, the demand for security jobs is also increasing. Dubai offers many security jobs that come with free visa and ticket for individuals who are willing to work in the security field. In this article, we will explore the different types of security jobs in Dubai and how you can apply for them.

Types of Security Jobs

Dubai offers a wide range of security jobs, including security guards, security supervisors, security officers, and many more. These jobs are available in various industries, such as hotels, malls, airports, and residential areas. Each of these jobs requires different skills and qualifications, and the salary also varies depending on the position.

Qualifications Required

To work in the security field in Dubai, you need to have a high school diploma or equivalent qualification. You also need to have excellent communication skills and be able to speak English fluently. Additionally, you need to have a valid security license issued by the Dubai Police Department.

How to Apply

To apply for a security job in Dubai, you need to visit the official websites of the companies that offer security jobs. You can also visit the job portals that list security jobs in Dubai. Once you find a job that suits your qualifications, you can apply for it online or submit your resume in person.

Salary and Benefits

The salary for security jobs in Dubai varies depending on the position and the company you work for. On average, security guards earn around 2,500 AED per month, while security officers earn around 3,500 AED per month. In addition to the salary, you also get free accommodation, transportation, and medical insurance. Some companies also offer annual leave, sick leave, and other benefits.

Working Conditions

Working in the security field in Dubai can be challenging, as you have to work long hours and deal with different types of people. You also have to work in different environments, such as outdoors and indoors, and you may have to work in shifts. However, the job is rewarding, as you get to protect people and property and contribute to the safety of the city.

Conclusion

Dubai offers many opportunities for people who are willing to work in the security field. If you have the qualifications and skills required, you can apply for a security job in Dubai and enjoy the benefits that come with it. Working in the security field in Dubai is not only a good career choice, but it also allows you to contribute to the safety and security of the city.
