Introduction

Dubai is a booming city with a growing demand for security personnel. However, the Security Industry Regulatory Agency (SIRA) is the governing body for security-related jobs in Dubai. Many people believe that it is impossible to work in security in Dubai without a SIRA license. In this article, we will discuss security jobs in Dubai without SIRA and how to get started in this field.

What is SIRA?

SIRA is the Security Industry Regulatory Agency, which was established in Dubai to regulate the security industry. It is mandatory for anyone working in security-related jobs in Dubai to obtain a SIRA license. However, not all security jobs require a SIRA license.

Security Jobs Without SIRA License

There are several security-related jobs in Dubai that do not require a SIRA license. These jobs include security guards in residential communities, office buildings, and hotels. These jobs require basic security training, and the employer may provide the necessary training.

Security Guards in Residential Communities

Security guards in residential communities are responsible for patrolling the area and ensuring the safety of the residents. They may also monitor CCTV cameras and respond to any security incidents. This job does not require a SIRA license, but the employer may require a basic security training certificate.

Office Building Security Guards

Office building security guards are responsible for ensuring the safety of the employees and visitors in the building. They may monitor CCTV cameras, check identification, and respond to any security incidents. This job also does not require a SIRA license, but the employer may require a basic security training certificate.

Hotel Security Guards

Hotel security guards are responsible for ensuring the safety of the guests and the hotel property. They may monitor CCTV cameras, check identification, and respond to any security incidents. This job does not require a SIRA license, but the employer may require a basic security training certificate.

How to Get Started in Security Jobs Without SIRA

To get started in security jobs without SIRA, you need to have a basic security training certificate. This certificate can be obtained from private security training institutes in Dubai. The course usually takes a few days to complete and covers topics such as security guard duties, emergency response, and communication skills.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are several security-related jobs in Dubai that do not require a SIRA license. These jobs include security guards in residential communities, office buildings, and hotels. To get started in these jobs, you need to have a basic security training certificate, which can be obtained from private security training institutes in Dubai. So, if you are interested in working in security in Dubai, there are options available for you even if you don't have a SIRA license.
