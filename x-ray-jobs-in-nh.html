Introduction

If you are looking for a career in healthcare, X Ray Jobs in NH can be an excellent option for you. X Ray Technicians or Radiologic Technologists are in high demand due to the increasing need for medical imaging. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about X Ray Jobs in NH.

Job Description

X Ray Technicians or Radiologic Technologists use X-ray machines to capture images of different parts of the human body. They work under the supervision of a Radiologist or a Physician to help diagnose medical conditions. X Ray Technicians can work in hospitals, clinics, diagnostic imaging centers, or private practices.

Education and Training

To become an X Ray Technician in NH, you need to complete a radiologic technology program that is accredited by the Joint Review Committee on Education in Radiologic Technology (JRCERT). The program typically takes two years to complete and includes both classroom and clinical training. After completing the program, you need to pass the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists (ARRT) exam to become a certified X Ray Technician. Some employers may also require additional certifications or licenses.

Skills and Qualities

X Ray Technicians need to have excellent communication skills to interact with patients and other healthcare professionals. They should also be detail-oriented, have good hand-eye coordination, and be able to work in a fast-paced environment. Additionally, X Ray Technicians should have a strong knowledge of anatomy, physiology, and medical terminology.

Salary and Job Outlook

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the median annual salary for X Ray Technicians in NH is $62,320 as of May 2023. The job outlook for X Ray Technicians is also positive, with an expected growth rate of 9% from 2022 to 2032.

Advancement Opportunities

X Ray Technicians can advance in their careers by specializing in a particular area of medical imaging, such as magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), computed tomography (CT), or mammography. They can also pursue further education to become a Radiologist Assistant or a Radiologist.

Job Search Tips

To find X Ray Jobs in NH, you can start by searching online job boards or visiting the websites of hospitals and diagnostic imaging centers in your area. You can also network with other healthcare professionals or attend job fairs to learn about job openings.

Conclusion

X Ray Jobs in NH can be a rewarding career choice for those interested in healthcare. With the increasing demand for medical imaging, the job outlook for X Ray Technicians is positive. If you are interested in pursuing a career in this field, make sure to complete an accredited radiologic technology program and obtain the necessary certifications.
